Why use Linux?

There are a lot of reason to why Linux is superior to other OSs

Absolutely Free

Linux is a free in aspects of both pricing and licensing. You can also customize it and re-distribute it for free.

Blessing for older systems

Linux can be configured in such ways that it can even run on 10+ year old devices.

Customization

Windows and macOS have some limitations on how much you can customize them, Linux on the other hand can be customize a lot. You can make it look like Windows, macOS or anything you want, literally.

Great community & documentation

When you are in need of help, you can get a variety of support in the form of documentation, forums and live chat.

Confused about which distro to use?

There are around hundreds of Linux distributions for different uses, but which one should you use? The answer depends on your use case and other things like are you using Linux for the first time or the hundredth time.
